Our Story

Born from Chaos

Egg United was formed when five people who had absolutely no business playing together decided to queue as a team anyway. The result was exactly what you'd expect — and also somehow greater than the sum of its parts.

We don't have a coach. We don't have a practice schedule. We don't agree on anything, including whether Trundle is a viable bot laner (he is not, and yet). What we do have is chemistry, trauma bonding, and a commitment to losing games in the most creative ways possible.

The egg isn't a symbol of fragility. It's a symbol of potential. Raw, chaotic, possibly already scrambled potential.
